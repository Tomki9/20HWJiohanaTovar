There is nothing like a good story.

Of course, we have limitations in budget, time, resources and this was more of an opportunity to be creative and flexible, to work with what we had. A valuable lesson to let go and adapt. More recently I earned a certificate in UX/UI design from the University of Central Florida (UCF).

My inner call of creation and storytelling was matched in a harmony I could have never predicted. This certificate taught me that I could use my creative thinking to produce user personas, for keeping the true north for how we are designing. My film degree prepared me to create unique storyboards, scenarios, and personas necessary to be empathetic to users. My curious side was delighted to learn how to prototype in Figma. I was surprised to see myself enjoying researching, testing and seeing the results of my work. My builder side was excited to learn the basics of code in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
